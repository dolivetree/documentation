# Supported units

Deci supports the following units, and compositions of them:

## Length units

- **meter**, m, metre
- inch, in
- foot, ft
- yard, yd
- mile, mi
- nauticalmile, nmi
- angstrom
- furlong
- smoot

## Volume units

- **cubicmeter**
- liter

## Pressure units

- **atmosphere**, atm
- Pascal, Pa
- Bar, Ba
- Torr, mmHg
- PSI

## Energy units

- **joule**
- calorie

## Mass units

- **gram**, gr, g
- pound
- ounce
- ton

## Temperature units

- **kelvin**, °K
- celsius, °C
- fahrenheit, °F

## Time units

- **second**
- millisecond
- minute
- hour
- day
- week
- month
- year

## Information units

- **bit**
- byte

## Power units

- **Watt**, W

## Substance units

- **mole**

## Electric current units

- **Ampere**, amp
